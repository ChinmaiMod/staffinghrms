# Timesheet Management - UI Design Specification

## ğŸ“‹ Executive Summary

The Timesheet Management module enables employees to track their working hours across projects, with support for daily, weekly, and monthly time tracking. Includes approval workflows, project-based time allocation, and reporting capabilities.

**Page URLs:**
- List: `/hrms/timesheets`
- Create: `/hrms/timesheets/new`
- Detail: `/hrms/timesheets/:timesheetId`
- Approve: `/hrms/timesheets/approve` (Manager view)

**Access Level:** All employees (own timesheets), Managers (team timesheets), HRMS Admin (all)  
**Primary Function:** Time tracking with approval workflow

**Security & Access Control:**
- Employees can only view/edit their own timesheets (RLS + server-side checks).
- Managers can view/approve only within their permitted team scope; HRMS Admin can view/act across tenant.
- Approval and status-change actions must be enforced server-side (do not rely on UI-only guards).

See [UI_DESIGN_DOCS/19_AUTHENTICATION_AUTHORIZATION_SECURITY.md](19_AUTHENTICATION_AUTHORIZATION_SECURITY.md).

### Timesheet Entry Validations

| Field | Validation | Error Message |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| Hours | Required, 0-24 per day | "Hours must be between 0 and 24" |
| Daily Total | Warning if >8 | "This entry exceeds 8 hours (overtime)" |
| Weekly Total | Warning if >40 | "Weekly hours exceed 40 (overtime)" |

| Period | Cannot be in future | "Cannot enter time for future dates" |
